■ Admission information

Application qualification
  • Graduate student in the Graduate School of Regional Development and Creativity at Utsunomiya University
  • Graduate student at partner universities
  • TOEIC score : 650 or higher
Enrollment capacity

FY2021 : 7 students
Language

English

*When students from Africa come to Japan, they can take a beginner level Japanese class.

Cost Students can apply for a scholarship given by JASSO and then receive monthly stipend of 60,000 to 100,000 yen and 160,000 yen as travel expenses.

■ FAQs

  • Q. Is there any financial support to apply this program?

    A. Students can apply for a scholarship given by JASSO and then receive monthly stipend of 60,000 to 100,000 yen and 160,000 yen as travel expenses.

  • Q. What is required to apply this program?

    A. You should belong to a graduate school at one of the universities below: Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology, Meru University of Science and Technology, Addis Ababa University, University of Dar es Salaam, Nelson Mandela African Institution of Science and Technology, University of Ghana

  • Q. Should I learn languages other than English?

    A. We only use English and Japanese. You don’t have to learn other languages.
